About me

I'm passionate about changing, improving and developing. To find new solutions and to create good conditions to learn and grow. New ways of working require new ways of learning. Rapid adaptability, employer branding, collaboration and sustainable solutions are and will be key factors. The ability to adapt to new conditions, changed gameplan and new roles will be crucial. Both for companies, organisations and employees. I want to contribute to the development of today's and tomorrow's ways of learning and transfer information. To meet the new demands, to skill, reskill and upskill and to motivate, inspire and engage. For great performance in worklife.

"Effective learning is based on business goals and is adapted for the user.
It provides the right knowledge, via the right channels, at the right time, at the right price".
