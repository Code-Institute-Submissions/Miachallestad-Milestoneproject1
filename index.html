Concept development

Next Office

A concept for how to create innovative and efficient office solutions including people, technology and the physical environment. Based on research and analysis. To create sustainable office solutions, increase creativity, strengthen the brand and for better health.

Background

Studies showed that the occupancy rate at the personal workstations was rarely more than 50%. Half (often more) of the workstations were always empty. The working life was undergoing a number of big changes. The most important driving forces behind the changes were new technology, new values and globalisation. The traditional workplace didn‚Äôt support all the activities that occur in the office. Often environments for different type of meetings, co-operation, creative work and high focus tasks were lacking. Nor did these environments usually take in to account the individual needs and driving forces of the employees. It was natural to look for new solutions that made more effective use of the workplace.

Scope

It started as external monitoring and ended up as a big project including a new sales process, showroom, new technology and change management. We went from being a company selling products to also offer services. It was a great challenge for the salesforce who became consultants in areas they haven‚Äôt worked with before. But it was very welcomed due to the fact that it opened up for a whole new way of selling furniture.

The future workplace

Everything indicates that the workplace of the future is flexible, location-independent and more activity-based. The workplace is configured in line with the actual workforce, it‚Äôs characterised by diversity and has more varied types of flexible, multi-functional and stimulating environments to better meet the needs of the employees. The choice of environment is based on task, personality, age, physical needs, culture, mood, colleagues, design etc. The personal desk is less important.
